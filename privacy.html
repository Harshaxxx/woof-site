Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 27, 2025

Woof is in pre-launch. This site is a preview of our product vision. We currently collect only the email you submit to join the waitlist.

What we collect

If you join the waitlist, you provide your email address so we can contact you about early access, updates, city launches, and partnership opportunities.

How we use it

We use your email to notify you when Woof becomes available in your area, or to follow up if you indicated investor / partner interest.

Who gets access

We do not sell or rent your data. We do not share your email with third parties except basic infrastructure services required to store and send email.

Data removal

You can request to be removed from the waitlist and deleted from our list at any time. Just email privacy@woof.cool.

Future features

The final Woof app will include optional location features for safety (meetup zones, Duo Check, temporary live location sharing). Location sharing will always be opt-in and automatically stops after the walk.

This document will evolve as the product launches publicly.
